'''Spider-UK''' is the name of two [[superhero]]es appearing in [[American comic book]]s published in [[Marvel Comics]].

The William Braddock version of Spider-UK is introduced as a British-based Spider-Man and the leader of the [[Web Warriors]].<ref>{{cite web|date=17 July 2017|title=Spider-Man: 28 Marvel Superheroes With Spider-Powers, Ranked|url=https://screenrant.com/spider-man-marvel-superheroes-with-spider-powers-ranked/|access-date=4 January 2018|website=Screen Rant}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Who Are All These Spider-People Slinging Around Marvel Comics?|url=https://www.inverse.com/article/15689-who-are-all-these-spider-people-a-guide-to-marvel-s-current-webslingers|access-date=4 January 2018|website=Inverse|language=en}}</ref>

==Publication history==
The William Braddock version of Spider-UK first appeared in ''Edge of Spider-Verse'' #2, as  one of the lead characters in the "[[Spider-Verse]]" storyline and was created by [[Jason Latour]] and Robbi Rodriguez.

The Zarina Zahari version of Spider-UK first appeared in ''Edge of Spider-Verse'' Vol. 2 #1 and was created by Ramzee and Zoe Thorogood.

==Fictional character biography==
===William Braddock===
Originating from Earth-833, William "Billy" Braddock is one of the newest recruits of the [[Captain Britain]] Corps who operates as Spider-UK. Scanning other universes during the "[[Spider-Verse]]" storyline, he has seen the deaths of alternate versions of [[Spider-Man]] by the [[Inheritors (comics)|Inheritors]]. Inheritor [[Jennix]] detects Billy watching them and immediately cuts the scanner feed. As [[Saturnyne]] and [[Roma (comics)|Lady Roma]] discuss their concerns about [[Secret Wars (2015 comic book)|the Incursions that are destroying many dimensions]], Spider-UK tries to explain about the killings of the Spiders across the multiverse. He gets rebuffed by Saturnyne, but a sympathetic Roma gives him a talisman that allows him to travel through the web of life in order to save the remaining Spiders.<ref>''The Amazing Spider-Man'' vol. 3 #7 (2014). Marvel Comics.</ref> 

Spider-UK was first seen having arrived in Earth-65 where he sees [[Spider-Woman (Gwen Stacy)|Spider-Woman]] as a possible recruit for the army of Spider-Men.<ref>''Edge of Spider-Verse'' #2. Marvel Comics.</ref> Spider-UK later accompanies [[Spider-Woman (Jessica Drew)|Spider-Woman]], [[Anya Corazon|Spider-Girl]], [[Spider-Man 2099]], [[Spider-Girl (Mayday Parker)|Spider-Girl of Earth-982]], and [[Spider-Ham]] into meeting with Spider-Man and [[Silk (comics)|Silk]]. Spider-UK explains they are the Spiders of other dimensions and that all the strands of the Great Web are converging on [[Earth-616]] Peter. Spider-UK tells him Morlun's elder brother Daemos is coming to Earth-616 and all of them head into a portal to another dimension. When on Earth-13, Spider-Man is told by Spider-UK and Earth-13's Spider-Man who never lost the [[Captain Universe|Enigma Force]] that there is a war coming and that Spider-Man is the greatest Spider-Man of all.<ref>''Amazing Spider-Man'' vol. 3 #9 (2014). Marvel Comics.</ref> It is explained by Spider-UK and Spider-Man of Earth-13 that Spider-Man is the only Spider-Man that faced an Inheritor and won. Spider-UK gets a reading on the second team and Old Man Spider-Man of Earth-4 says they need to act quickly to recruit them.<ref>''Amazing Spider-Man'' vol. 3 #10 (2014). Marvel Comics.</ref> 

When Morlun and his father [[Solus (Marvel Comics)|Solus]] attack Earth-13, Spider-UK calls Spider-Man and tells him the Safe Zone is lost.<ref>''Amazing Spider-Man'' vol. 3 #12 (2014). Marvel Comics.</ref> Kwaku Anansi of Earth-7082 is a spider totem that is recruited by Spider-UK. Before he can join, Anansi needs to trick a demi-god Mister Mighty after stealing his sheep, then escape from Shungo the Storm God. On Earth-TRN521, Spider-UK recruits the Mexican Spider-Man named Arácnido.<ref>''Spider-Verse'' #2. Marvel Comics.</ref> On Earth-3123, Karn is stopped from feeding on Spider-Ma'am by Spider-Girl, Spider-UK, Spider-Punk, [[Spider-Man: India|Spider-Man of India]], and [[Spider-Girl (Mayday Parker)|Spider-Woman of Earth-807128]].<ref>''Spider-Verse Team-Up'' #3. Marvel Comics.</ref>

Spider-UK became a sole survivor from Earth-833 after his home reality was destroyed during the ''Time Runs Out'' storyline and chose to remain in the reality of the defeated Inheritors along with Anya Corazon.<ref>''The Amazing Spider-Man'' vol. 3 #15 (2015). Marvel Comics.</ref>

During the second volume of ''[[Spider-Verse]]'' set during the ''[[Secret Wars (2015 comic book)|Secret Wars]]'' event, Spider-UK and Anya Corazon found themselves in the [[Battleworld]] domain of Arachnia with no memories of how they got there.<ref>''Spider-Verse'' vol. 2 #1 (May. 2015). Marvel Comics. </ref> They eventually teamed up with Spider-Woman of Earth-65, Spider-Ham, [[Spider-Man Noir]], and Spider-Man of India with neither of them remembering their previous encounter during the original ''Spider-Verse''. After Gwen Stacy meets with the other spider-based heroes after her visit to [[Oscorp]], they are interrupted by the arrival of the [[Sinister Six]] (consisting of [[Doctor Octopus]], [[Electro (Marvel Comics)|Electro]], [[Kraven the Hunter]], [[Sandman (Marvel Comics)|Sandman]], [[Mac Gargan|Scorpion]], and [[Vulture (Marvel Comics)|Vulture]]).<ref>''Spider-Verse'' vol. 2 #2 (Jun. 2015). Marvel Comics.</ref> The Sinister Six defeat the Spider-Men and bring them to Mayor [[Norman Osborn]] who states he wants to help them. This causes Spider-UK and the other Spider-Men to linger around Oscorp for a while.<ref>''Spider-Verse'' vol. 2 #3 (2015). Marvel Comics.</ref> Spider-UK and the other Spider-Men join Spider-Woman of Earth-65 and Arachnia's Spider-Man into defeating Norman Osborn and the Sinister Six.<ref>''Spider-Verse'' vol. 2 #5 (2015). Marvel Comics.</ref>

Following the conclusion of ''Secret Wars'' the team of six Spiders will rename itself and feature in a new ongoing series called ''Web Warriors'', a name that was coined by Peter Parker from the [[Ultimate Spider-Man (TV series)|''Ultimate Spider-Man'' TV series]] during the original ''Spider-Verse''.<ref name="Web Warriors">{{cite web |first=Rich |last=Johnston |author-link=Rich Johnston |url=http://www.bleedingcool.com/2015/06/29/spider-gwen-stars-in-web-warriors-launched-by-mike-costa-and-david-baldeon-marveloctober/ |title=Spider-Gwen Stars In Web Warriors Launched By Mike Costa And David Baldeon #MarvelOctober (UPDATE) |publisher=[[Bleeding Cool]] |date=29 June 2015 |access-date=29 August 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304001923/http://www.bleedingcool.com/2015/06/29/spider-gwen-stars-in-web-warriors-launched-by-mike-costa-and-david-baldeon-marveloctober/|archive-date=March 4, 2016}}</ref>

During the "[[Spider-Geddon]]" storyline, Spider-UK has been keeping surveillance on Earth-3145 with the help of Spider-Girl, Spider-Punk, [[Spider-Man (Pavitr Prabhakar)|Spider-Man India]], and Master Weaver. They find that the Inheritors have become malnourished since they were last seen.<ref>''Spider-Geddon'' #0. Marvel Comics.</ref> While Loomworld was being dismantled, Spider-UK talks to Master Weaver on how the other spider-based heroes have a world to go to while he lost his world during the Incursions as they both consider each other greatest friends. The two of them get alerted to an Inheritor Cloning Engine on Earth-616. The two of them figure out what is happening. On Earth-3145, Jennix has put the Spider-Bot heads to good use by harvesting their transmitters. As they are unable to send a kill signal to the Inheritor Cloning Engine, Spider-UK advises Master Weaver to call the Web Warriors. As Master Weaver states that nobody can fight the Inheritors and win, Spider-UK states that they'll die trying to keep another world from being destroyed.<ref>''Superior Octopus'' #1. Marvel Comics.</ref> Spider-UK is among the Web Warriors that arrive on Earth-616 and take Miles to confront [[Doctor Octopus#Superior Octopus|Superior Octopus]]. When Morlun emerges from the cloning machine, Spider-UK and Superior Octopus fight Morlun until Verna emerges and snaps Spider-UK's neck to keep Morlun from being harmed.<ref>''Spider-Geddon'' #1. Marvel Comics.</ref>

After the alternate Spider-Men defeat the Inheritors again, Billy's body is laid to rest on Earth-803, the home of Lady Spider.<ref>''Spider-Gwen: Ghost Spider'' #4. Marvel Comics.</ref>

===Zarina Zahari===
In a lead-up to the "[[End of the Spider-Verse]]" storyline, Zarina Zahari is a young girl and [[gamer]] living in Earth-834's London who gained spider-like abilities and joined the organization W.H.O. (short for Weird Happenings Organization) as this reality's Spider-UK. After besting a [[wyvern]], she was approached by [[Julia Carpenter|Madame Web]] to help fight a Multiversal threat that is going to target the spider-powered characters.<ref>''Edge of Spider-Verse'' Vol. 2 #1. Marvel Comics.</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
The William Braddock version of Spider-UK has powers similar to Spider-Man which include sticking and climbing on walls, a sixth sense to danger–which he struggles with–and being able to lift approximately 10 tons. He also has his own web-shooters. Spider-UK also makes use of an inter-dimensional device that enables him to travel to different dimensions.

The Zarina Zahari version of Spider-UK can generate bio-electricity where she wears special earrings to keep her bio-electrical abilities in line.
